Our Story

The Skinny Co. understand that a healthy workforce equates to a happier and more productive workforce. All our meals and shakes are made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients and guided by professional nutrionists so you can be gauranteed a well balanced meal. We pride ourselves on having something for everyone, no matter what your dietary requirements.

Being socially resonsible and eco friendly is at the heart of everything we do. All our packaging and utensils are biodegradable and none of our food goes to waste. Anything we don't sell is given to local foodbanks.

Meet The Team

The Skinny Co. was founded in 2018 by Danielle Smyth & Holly Steele-Nicholson, Ulster University Graduates with a passion for improving workplace health.

"Poor diet in the workplace has significant impact on physical and mental well being and we want to change that." - Holly Steele-Nicholson

"We are both Friends of the Earth and want to be the example for other food outlets and retailers in reducing the carbon footprints of this sector." - Danielle Smyth
